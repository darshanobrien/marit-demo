# User Stories

## Business User

- As a Business User, I want to submit a business pain point so the AI Lab can evaluate whether AI could help.
- As a Business User, I want guidance in the intake form so I know what details are useful.
- As a Business User, I want clear demo warnings so I do not enter real client, confidential, restricted, or personal data.
- As a Business User, I want to load a fictional sample request so I can demonstrate the intake flow quickly.
- As a Business User, I want a confirmation after submission so I know the mock assessment is ready for AI Builder review.
- As a Business User, I want to see my submitted business cases so I know what has been captured.
- As a Business User, I want a plain-language status so I know whether my idea is awaiting review, under review, or reviewed.
- As a Business User, I want a simple next step so I know what happens after the mock assessment is generated.

## AI Builder

- As an AI Builder, I want to see a prioritized list of submitted opportunities so I can focus on the most promising cases.
- As an AI Builder, I want filters and sorting so I can quickly find urgent, ready, or high-priority requests.
- As an AI Builder, I want lightweight tool-fit and responsible AI risk signals in the list before opening a full report.
- As an AI Builder, I want to open an AI-generated assessment report so I can review feasibility, risks, and tool fit.
- As an AI Builder, I want responsible AI pillar scores with explanations so I can identify concerns early.
- As an AI Builder, I want to mark an assessment as reviewed so the team can distinguish AI-generated draft content from human-reviewed guidance.
- As an AI Builder, I want to add review notes so follow-up discovery work is clear.

## Admin

- As an Admin, I want future configuration boundaries documented so production settings can be added later.
- As an Admin, I may eventually manage the mock AI tools catalogue, but this is not required for the first demo.

## Cross-Cutting Stories

- As any user, I want to use the app in English or Canadian French.
- As any keyboard user, I want every interaction to work without a mouse.
- As any user, I want risk, status, and priority to be conveyed with text and structure, not colour alone.
- As a stakeholder, I want the demo to run cheaply and reliably.
