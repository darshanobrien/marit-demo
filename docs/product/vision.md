# Product Vision

## What Marit Is

Marit is an AI Business Case Prioritization and Evaluation Tool. It helps business teams explore whether AI could address a pain point, while helping an internal AI Lab prioritize and review opportunities.

## Why It Exists

Business teams often have AI ideas before they know whether those ideas are feasible, safe, affordable, or aligned to available internal tooling. AI Builders need a fast way to understand the opportunity, risks, likely fit, and responsible AI implications before spending prototyping time.

Marit creates a structured intake and assessment loop so the AI Lab can review more ideas with less manual triage.

Administrators maintain an inventory of approved, restricted, pilot, and deprecated AI tools, as well as AI tools currently under review. Business cases are assessed not just abstractly, but against the actual AI capabilities available to the firm.

## First Demo Outcome

The first demo should prove that Marit can:

- Explain the product and workflow clearly through a bilingual landing page.
- Capture a business pain point through a structured intake form.
- Compare the request with a mocked catalogue of available AI tools.
- Generate a useful assessment report for AI Builders.
- Score the idea against 10 responsible AI pillars.
- Let an AI Builder review the AI-generated assessment.

## Success Criteria

- A stakeholder can understand the workflow in under five minutes.
- A stakeholder can understand what Marit is before seeing the app shell.
- The demo uses mock data only.
- The assessment report feels specific enough to discuss.
- The responsible AI section is clear, practical, and reviewable.
- The app is inexpensive to host and easy to reset.

## Out of Scope for First Demo

- Real authentication.
- Real client data.
- Production persistence.
- Automated approvals.
- Agentic AI workflows.
- Enterprise integration.
- Full Azure infrastructure automation.
